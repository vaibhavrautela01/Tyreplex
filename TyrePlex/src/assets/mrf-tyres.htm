      About MRF

      A prominent brand of our country, MRF (Madras Rubber Factory Limited) tyres have gained worldwide repute. In 1993, the company became the first tyre company in India to cross the INR 10 Billion mark. MRF has won the JD Power award along with the TNS and CAPEXIL award for being the most reputed tyre company in the country. MRF is a leading name on the map of the global tyre industry, and customers have been trusting the brand since the day it launched its first product in the industry. The brand manufactures two-wheeler, four-wheeler tyres and tyres for trucks or buses and is well-recognized for outstanding and optimum quality.

      MRF Tyre FAQs

      There are 142 MRF Tyres available, out of which 68 can be fitted into car, 64 can be fitted in bike and 21 can be fitted into scooter.

      There are 18 tyre series in MRF which are MUSCLEROK, NYLOGRIP ZAPPER, NYLOGRIP PLUS, NYLOGRIP, NYLOGRIP MOTO, NYLOGRIP EZEERIDE, SAVARI, STEEL MUSCLE, SUPERLUG, SHAKTI, TRACTOR, WANDERER, SUPERTREKKER, ESTATE, MASSETER, METEOR, REVZ and ZAPPER.

      The lowest-priced tyre in MRF is MRF NYLOGRIP and available for Rs.900 and the highest priced tyre is MRF WANDERER A/T OWL for Rs.15050.

      MRF are available in Tube Type and Tubeless.
